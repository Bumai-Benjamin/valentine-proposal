My Love,

I sit here thinking about you and somehow every thought turns into a smile. You are my favorite feeling my safest place and my best surprise in a world that moves too fast you slow me down and remind me to breathe. With you love feels easy, warm and a little silly like laughing at nothing and everything at once. You are beautiful in the way the sun is beautiful, not because it tries but because it just is. When I am with you even my bad jokes feel funnier and my worries forget their lines, you turn ordinary days into stories I want to tell forever.
